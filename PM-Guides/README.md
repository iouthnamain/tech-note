# Project Management Guides

Welcome to the comprehensive collection of project management guides. These guides cover all aspects of modern project management, from methodologies and planning to team leadership and delivery.

## Overview

This collection contains 7 comprehensive guides designed for project managers and those aspiring to become PMs. Each guide is production-ready, includes practical templates and examples, and covers both theory and real-world applications.

## Prerequisites

### Beginner Level
- Basic understanding of project management concepts
- Familiarity with software development lifecycle
- Basic communication skills

### Intermediate Level
- 1-2 years of project management experience
- Understanding of different project methodologies
- Experience with team collaboration
- Basic knowledge of project management tools

### Senior Level (Target Audience)
- 3+ years of project management experience
- Experience managing multiple projects
- Understanding of both Waterfall and Agile methodologies
- Experience with stakeholder management
- Knowledge of risk management
