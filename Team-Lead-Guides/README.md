# Team Lead Guides

Welcome to the comprehensive collection of Team Lead guides. These guides cover all aspects of technical leadership, from code reviews and architecture to mentoring and team development.

## Overview

This collection contains 14 comprehensive guides designed for Team Leads (Tech Leads) and those aspiring to become technical leaders. Each guide is production-ready, includes practical examples and templates, and covers both technical and leadership aspects.

## Prerequisites

### Beginner Level
- 2-3 years of software development experience
- Basic understanding of software development lifecycle
- Interest in leadership and mentoring
- Good communication skills

### Intermediate Level
- 3-5 years of development experience
- Experience with code reviews
- Some mentoring experience
- Understanding of architecture and design

### Senior Level (Target Audience)
- 5+ years of development experience
- Experience leading technical initiatives
- Strong technical skills
- Leadership aspirations or current team lead role
