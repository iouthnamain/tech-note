# RISK-002 - Integration with Existing MM Processes

## Description

Integration with existing goods movement, purchasing, and inventory processes may be more complex than expected, impacting schedule.

## Response Strategy

**Strategy**: Mitigate

**Response Plan**:
- Involve MM functional consultant early in design
- Schedule integration design workshops with process owners
- Prototype key integrations before full build

**Contingency Plan**:
- De-scope non-critical integrations for phase 2

**Trigger**:
- Integration design not approved by end of Week 4
