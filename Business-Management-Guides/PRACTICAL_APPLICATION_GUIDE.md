## Introduction

This guide provides three comprehensive mock business projects that apply all management concepts covered in the Business Management Guides. Each project covers different industries and scenarios, allowing you to practice strategic thinking, planning, and decision-making.

Hướng dẫn này cung cấp ba dự án kinh doanh mô phỏng toàn diện áp dụng tất cả các khái niệm quản lý được đề cập trong Hướng dẫn Quản lý Kinh doanh. Mỗi dự án bao gồm các ngành và tình huống khác nhau, cho phép bạn thực hành tư duy chiến lược, lập kế hoạch và ra quyết định.

### Who This Guide Is For
- Business management students
- Aspiring managers
- Entrepreneurs
- Anyone applying management concepts
- Professionals seeking practical experience

### Key Learning Objectives
- Apply management fundamentals
- Practice strategic planning
- Develop business plans
- Make management decisions
- Integrate multiple management disciplines
- Evaluate business scenarios

### How to Use This Guide
1. Read each mock project scenario
2. Complete the exercises and questions
3. Develop solutions using management frameworks
4. Compare with provided solutions
5. Reflect on learning outcomes

## Mock Project 1: Tech Startup Launch

### Project Overview / Tổng quan dự án

**Scenario**: You are the founder of a tech startup developing a B2B SaaS project management tool. You have a working MVP, initial customer interest, and need to launch and scale the business.

**Tình huống**: Bạn là người sáng lập một công ty khởi nghiệp công nghệ phát triển công cụ quản lý dự án B2B SaaS. Bạn có MVP hoạt động, sự quan tâm ban đầu từ khách hàng và cần khởi động và mở rộng quy mô kinh doanh.

### Company Profile / Hồ sơ công ty

- **Company Name**: ProManage Solutions
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS - Project Management Software
- **Stage**: Early stage, pre-revenue
- **Team**: 5 people (2 founders, 2 developers, 1 designer)
- **Product**: Cloud-based project management tool for small-medium businesses
- **Market**: Small-medium businesses (10-100 employees) in North America
- **Competition**: Asana, Monday.com, Trello (established players)

### Current Situation / Tình huống hiện tại

**Strengths**:
- Working MVP with core features
- 20 beta customers providing positive feedback
- Strong technical team
- Clear product vision

**Challenges**:
- Limited funding ($50K from founders)
- No marketing expertise
- Need to acquire paying customers
- Competition from established players
- Need to scale team

### Project Exercises / Bài tập dự án

#### Exercise 1: Strategic Planning / Lập kế hoạch chiến lược

**Task**: Develop a 3-year strategic plan for ProManage Solutions.

**Requirements**:
1. Conduct SWOT analysis
2. Define vision and mission
3. Set strategic objectives (3-year, 1-year)
4. Develop competitive strategy
5. Create implementation roadmap

**Deliverables**:
- SWOT analysis document
- Strategic plan document
- Implementation timeline

#### Exercise 2: Business Plan Development / Phát triển kế hoạch kinh doanh

**Task**: Create comprehensive business plan for investor pitch.

**Requirements**:
1. Executive summary
2. Market analysis (size, trends, competition)
3. Product description and competitive advantages
4. Marketing and sales strategy
5. Operations plan
6. Management team overview
7. Financial projections (3 years)
8. Funding requirements

**Deliverables**:
- Complete business plan (15-20 pages)
- Financial model (Excel)
- Pitch deck (10-12 slides)

#### Exercise 3: Marketing Strategy / Chiến lược marketing

**Task**: Develop go-to-market strategy for product launch.

**Requirements**:
1. Target market segmentation
2. Positioning strategy
3. Marketing mix (4Ps)
4. Digital marketing plan
5. Budget allocation ($50K marketing budget)
6. Customer acquisition strategy
7. Metrics and KPIs

**Deliverables**:
- Marketing plan document
- Marketing calendar
- Budget breakdown

#### Exercise 4: Financial Planning / Lập kế hoạch tài chính

**Task**: Create financial model and funding strategy.

**Requirements**:
1. Startup costs breakdown
2. Revenue projections (3 years)
3. Expense forecasts
4. Cash flow projections
5. Break-even analysis
6. Funding requirements and use of funds
7. Valuation considerations

**Deliverables**:
- Financial model (Excel)
- Funding proposal
- Investor presentation

#### Exercise 5: Operations and Team Building / Vận hành và xây dựng đội ngũ

**Task**: Plan operations and team expansion.

**Requirements**:
1. Organizational structure
2. Hiring plan (next 12 months)
3. Key processes and systems
4. Technology infrastructure
5. Quality assurance
6. Customer support plan

**Deliverables**:
- Organizational plan
- Job descriptions
- Process documentation

### Solution Framework / Khung giải pháp

**Strategic Approach**:
1. **Positioning**: Focus on specific niche (e.g., creative agencies, consulting firms)
2. **Differentiation**: Emphasize ease of use and affordability
3. **Go-to-Market**: Digital-first, content marketing, freemium model
4. **Funding**: Seek $500K seed round for 18-month runway
5. **Growth**: Focus on customer acquisition and retention

**Key Decisions**:
- Target niche market initially
- Freemium pricing model
- Content marketing strategy
- Hire sales and marketing head
- Raise seed funding within 6 months

## Mock Project 2: Manufacturing Company Transformation

### Project Overview / Tổng quan dự án

**Scenario**: You are the operations manager of a mid-size manufacturing company facing quality issues, high costs, and declining market share. The CEO has tasked you with leading a transformation initiative.

**Tình huống**: Bạn là giám đốc vận hành của một công ty sản xuất quy mô trung bình đang gặp vấn đề về chất lượng, chi phí cao và thị phần giảm. CEO đã giao cho bạn dẫn dắt một sáng kiến chuyển đổi.

### Company Profile / Hồ sơ công ty

- **Company Name**: Precision Manufacturing Inc.
- **Industry**: Automotive parts manufacturing
- **Size**: 200 employees, $25M annual revenue
- **Location**: Midwest USA
- **Products**: Precision metal components for automotive industry
- **Customers**: Tier 1 automotive suppliers
- **Market**: Competitive, price-sensitive, quality-critical

### Current Situation / Tình huống hiện tại

**Problems**:
- Defect rate: 5% (industry average: 2%)
- On-time delivery: 75% (target: 95%)
- Customer complaints increasing
- High employee turnover (25% annually)
- Declining profit margins
- Lost 2 major customers in past year

**Strengths**:
- Experienced workforce
- Good customer relationships (some)
- Modern equipment (recently upgraded)
- Strong technical capabilities

**Opportunities**:
- Growing electric vehicle market
- New customer segments
- Process improvement potential
- Technology adoption

### Project Exercises / Bài tập dự án

#### Exercise 1: Operations Analysis / Phân tích vận hành

**Task**: Conduct comprehensive operations analysis and identify improvement opportunities.

**Requirements**:
1. Process mapping (value stream mapping)
2. Identify waste and inefficiencies
3. Root cause analysis of quality issues
4. Capacity analysis
5. Supply chain assessment
6. Quality system review

**Deliverables**:
- Process maps
- Root cause analysis report
- Improvement opportunity list
- Priority matrix

#### Exercise 2: Quality Management Implementation / Triển khai quản lý chất lượng

**Task**: Develop and implement quality improvement program.

**Requirements**:
1. Quality improvement strategy
2. Six Sigma implementation plan
3. Quality control procedures
4. Employee training program
5. Quality metrics and monitoring
6. Continuous improvement process

**Deliverables**:
- Quality management plan
- Training program
- Quality control procedures
- Metrics dashboard

#### Exercise 3: Change Management / Quản lý thay đổi

**Task**: Develop change management plan for transformation.

**Requirements**:
1. Change vision and objectives
2. Stakeholder analysis
3. Communication plan
4. Resistance management strategy
5. Training and development plan
6. Change metrics

**Deliverables**:
- Change management plan
- Communication materials
- Training schedule
- Change readiness assessment

#### Exercise 4: Strategic Repositioning / Định vị lại chiến lược

**Task**: Develop strategic plan to reposition company.

**Requirements**:
1. Market analysis (PEST, Five Forces)
2. Competitive analysis
3. Strategic options evaluation
4. New market opportunities (EV market)
5. Strategic plan
6. Implementation roadmap

**Deliverables**:
- Strategic analysis
- Strategic plan
- Market entry plan (if applicable)
- Implementation plan

#### Exercise 5: Financial Turnaround / Chuyển đổi tài chính

**Task**: Develop financial improvement plan.

**Requirements**:
1. Cost analysis and reduction plan
2. Pricing strategy review
3. Profitability improvement
4. Cash flow optimization
5. Investment priorities
6. Financial projections

**Deliverables**:
- Cost reduction plan
- Financial improvement plan
- Investment proposal
- Financial projections

### Solution Framework / Khung giải pháp

**Transformation Strategy**:
1. **Phase 1 (Months 1-3)**: Stabilize operations, reduce defects
2. **Phase 2 (Months 4-6)**: Implement Lean/Six Sigma, improve processes
3. **Phase 3 (Months 7-12)**: Strategic repositioning, new market entry
4. **Phase 4 (Year 2)**: Scale improvements, growth

**Key Initiatives**:
- Implement Six Sigma program (target: <1% defects)
- Lean manufacturing principles
- Employee engagement program
- Enter EV market segment
- Customer relationship improvement
- Technology upgrade (Industry 4.0)

**Expected Outcomes**:
- Defect rate: 5% → 1%
- On-time delivery: 75% → 95%
- Employee turnover: 25% → 10%
- Profit margin: +5 percentage points
- New revenue: $5M from EV market

## Mock Project 3: Retail Chain Expansion

### Project Overview / Tổng quan dự án

**Scenario**: You are the expansion manager for a successful regional retail chain. The company wants to expand nationally, entering 5 new markets over 3 years. You need to plan and execute the expansion.

**Tình huống**: Bạn là giám đốc mở rộng cho một chuỗi bán lẻ khu vực thành công. Công ty muốn mở rộng ra toàn quốc, thâm nhập 5 thị trường mới trong 3 năm. Bạn cần lập kế hoạch và thực hiện việc mở rộng.

### Company Profile / Hồ sơ công ty

- **Company Name**: GreenLife Retail
- **Industry**: Health and wellness retail
- **Current**: 15 stores in 3 states (West Coast)
- **Revenue**: $45M annually
- **Products**: Organic foods, supplements, wellness products
- **Positioning**: Premium, health-conscious consumers
- **Competition**: Whole Foods, Sprouts, local health stores

### Current Situation / Tình huống hiện tại

**Strengths**:
- Strong brand in current markets
- Profitable operations
- Loyal customer base
- Experienced management team
- Proven business model

**Expansion Goals**:
- Enter 5 new markets (East Coast, Midwest)
- Open 25 new stores over 3 years
- Maintain brand consistency
- Achieve profitability in new markets within 18 months
- Build national brand presence

**Challenges**:
- Different market dynamics in new regions
- Supply chain expansion
- Talent acquisition in new markets
- Competition from established players
- Capital requirements ($50M)

### Project Exercises / Bài tập dự án

#### Exercise 1: Market Entry Strategy / Chiến lược thâm nhập thị trường

**Task**: Develop market entry strategy for 5 new markets.

**Requirements**:
1. Market selection criteria
2. Market analysis for each target market
3. Entry strategy (phased approach)
4. Market prioritization
5. Entry timeline
6. Risk assessment

**Deliverables**:
- Market analysis report
- Market entry plan
- Risk assessment
- Timeline and milestones

#### Exercise 2: Financial Planning / Lập kế hoạch tài chính

**Task**: Develop financial plan for expansion.

**Requirements**:
1. Expansion budget ($50M allocation)
2. Store opening costs
3. Operating expenses for new stores
4. Revenue projections
5. Cash flow projections
6. Funding strategy
7. ROI analysis

**Deliverables**:
- Financial model
- Budget allocation
- Funding plan
- ROI analysis

#### Exercise 3: Operations Scaling / Mở rộng quy mô vận hành

**Task**: Plan operations for national expansion.

**Requirements**:
1. Supply chain expansion
2. Distribution network
3. Store operations standardization
4. Technology infrastructure
5. Quality control systems
6. Standard operating procedures

**Deliverables**:
- Operations plan
- Supply chain strategy
- SOP documentation
- Technology plan

#### Exercise 4: Marketing and Branding / Marketing và thương hiệu

**Task**: Develop national marketing strategy.

**Requirements**:
1. Brand positioning (national)
2. Marketing strategy for new markets
3. Local market adaptation
4. Digital marketing plan
5. Traditional marketing
6. Brand consistency
7. Marketing budget

**Deliverables**:
- Marketing strategy
- Brand guidelines
- Marketing calendar
- Budget allocation

#### Exercise 5: Human Resources / Nguồn nhân lực

**Task**: Plan HR strategy for expansion.

**Requirements**:
1. Organizational structure
2. Hiring plan (store managers, staff)
3. Training and onboarding
4. Compensation strategy
5. Culture maintenance
6. Talent acquisition strategy

**Deliverables**:
- HR strategy
- Hiring plan
- Training program
- Organizational chart

### Solution Framework / Khung giải pháp

**Expansion Strategy**:
1. **Phase 1 (Year 1)**: Enter 2 markets, open 8 stores
2. **Phase 2 (Year 2)**: Enter 2 more markets, open 10 stores
3. **Phase 3 (Year 3)**: Enter 1 final market, open 7 stores

**Market Selection**:
- Prioritize markets with similar demographics
- Focus on health-conscious, affluent areas
- Avoid oversaturated markets initially

**Key Success Factors**:
- Maintain brand consistency
- Local market adaptation
- Strong local partnerships
- Efficient supply chain
- Quality customer experience
- Experienced local teams

**Expected Outcomes**:
- 25 new stores in 5 markets
- $120M annual revenue (Year 3)
- National brand recognition
- Profitable in all markets by Year 2
- Market leadership in 2-3 markets

## Cross-Project Learning

### Common Themes / Chủ đề chung

Across all three projects, several management themes emerge:

1. **Strategic Thinking** - All projects require strategic planning
2. **Financial Management** - Financial planning and control are critical
3. **Operations Excellence** - Efficient operations drive success
4. **Change Management** - Managing change is essential
5. **Market Understanding** - Deep market knowledge is required
6. **Team Building** - Strong teams execute strategies
7. **Risk Management** - Identifying and mitigating risks

### Learning Outcomes / Kết quả học tập

After completing these projects, you should be able to:

1. **Apply Management Frameworks** - Use SWOT, PEST, Five Forces, etc.
2. **Develop Strategic Plans** - Create comprehensive strategic plans
3. **Create Business Plans** - Write investor-ready business plans
4. **Plan Financially** - Develop financial models and projections
5. **Manage Operations** - Design efficient operations
6. **Lead Change** - Plan and execute change initiatives
7. **Make Decisions** - Apply decision-making frameworks
8. **Integrate Disciplines** - Combine multiple management areas

## Evaluation Criteria

### Project Evaluation / Đánh giá dự án

#### Strategic Planning (25%)
- Quality of analysis (SWOT, market, competition)
- Strategic thinking and vision
- Realistic objectives
- Implementation feasibility

#### Business Planning (20%)
- Completeness of business plan
- Market understanding
- Financial projections quality
- Risk assessment

#### Operations Planning (20%)
- Process design quality
- Efficiency considerations
- Quality management
- Scalability

#### Financial Management (15%)
- Financial model accuracy
- Budget planning
- Cash flow management
- ROI analysis

#### Implementation Planning (10%)
- Actionability of plans
- Timeline realism
- Resource allocation
- Risk mitigation

#### Presentation Quality (10%)
- Clarity of communication
- Professional presentation
- Logical flow
- Supporting evidence

### Self-Assessment Questions / Câu hỏi tự đánh giá

After completing each project, ask yourself:

1. Did I apply management frameworks correctly?
2. Are my assumptions realistic?
3. Have I considered all key factors?
4. Is my plan actionable?
5. Have I identified and addressed risks?
6. Are my financial projections reasonable?
7. Can this plan be implemented?
8. What would I do differently?
